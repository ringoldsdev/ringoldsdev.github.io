### Galaxies

Galaxies are sprawling systems of stars, stellar remnants, interstellar gas, dust, and dark matter, bound together by gravity. Our own galaxy, the Milky Way, is just one of billions.

#### Types of Galaxies:

- Spiral (like the Milky Way and Andromeda)
- Elliptical
- Irregular

### Stars

Stars are luminous spheres of plasma held together by their own gravity. The nearest star to Earth is the Sun.
